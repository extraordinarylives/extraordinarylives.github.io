Help us reach our goal

Volunteer

We are always in need of volunteers for our events. We organize a High-5 Carnival every year and awareness events at Major League Baseball games and other fun activites.

Please send us an email letting us know how you would like to help.

Buy a Book

“The Power of Piggie Bear” is a children’s book written by Mara James, the founder of Extraordinary Lives.

Proceeds from sales of the book benefit the foundation and allow us to donate copies to children and schools.

Financial contributions are the simplest way to help us. We currently rely on generous donations to continue building awareness and making change.

Consider asking friends to donate to the foundation instead of a birthday gift this year. Every little bit makes a difference.
