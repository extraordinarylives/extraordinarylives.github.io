High-5 Carnival 2018

April 22, 2018

A big thank you to everyone who played a part in making this such a great day. For the first time, we had Piggie Bear in attendance who was a great hit with young and old. There were carnival games, balloons and face painting for the the little ones. Adults visited the many booths to explore alternative healing options and find support from many mental health support groups.

The music was spectacular! A beautiful rendition of the National Anthem by Kaitlyn Wright brought a tear to the eye. DJ Julien kept the beats coming and entertained us with his vocals. The soloists were awesome and we are sure you will see some of the the kids from the Center Stage Studio performing on Broadway in a few years time. Amazing talent!
